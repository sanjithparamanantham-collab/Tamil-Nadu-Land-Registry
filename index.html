Tamil Nadu Land Registry

உங்கள் நிலத்தை பதிவு செய்யுங்கள்

A transparent, open platform for farmers and buyers to register, discover, and understand agricultural land across Tamil Nadu.

🗺️

Interactive Map

Draw your land boundary on the Tamil Nadu map using polygon or rectangle tools. Area is auto-calculated.

💧

Water Source Details

Record well depth, bore well depth, canal irrigation, and river proximity for accurate water information.

🌱

Soil & Crop Info

Document soil type, current crop, previous crop history, and land fertility for buyers and planners.

🌐

Public Registry

All registered lands are visible to buyers, planners, and the next generation. No hidden data.

📍

Locality Details

Add village, taluk, district, nearby town, road access, and electricity availability.

📱

Mobile Friendly

Access and register land from any smartphone, tablet, or computer — no special software needed.
